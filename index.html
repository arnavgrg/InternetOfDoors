Internet Of Doors
Group 12
Instructions

Step 1
Enter the code below into the terminal, then hit the enter key.
./project r01.cs.ucla.edu 12000

Step 2
Press and hold the button when you are ready.

Step 3
While pressing the button, hold the light sensor in your other hand.

Step 4
You can input a 0,1 or 2 using the instructions below:
0: Partially cover the light sensor
       1: Expose the light sensor completely
     2: Cover the light sensor completely

Step 5
Release button to input the character.

Step 6
Repeat step 2-5 fives times to enter all 5 characters of the password.

Step 7
Repeat steps 2-6 if you entered the wrong password.
